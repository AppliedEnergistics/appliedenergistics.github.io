Portable Cell

The 1k Portable Item Cell is a portable inventory. It requires power to function but can be charged in the Charger. It acts like any other storage cell except that you can access its content without external hardware. It functions with the ME IO Port and other features in the same way as 1k ME Item Storage Cell or other storage cells.
1k Portable Item Cell are smaller then most cells, only carrying 512 bytes, and 27 types at 8 bytes per type. Its battery can hold 20k ae and it drains 0.5 ae/t when in a ME Drive or ME Chest however drains 1 ae/t when opened it directly.
