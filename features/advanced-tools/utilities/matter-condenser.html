Matter Condenser

Has two key functions, ellimination of excess materials in a cheap manor, and the production of Singularity and
Matter Ball.
Can accept both fluids and items to be destroyed. Each item or fluid will count as one additional energy. This energy can be stored on 1k ME Item Storage Component, 4k ME Item Storage Component, 16k ME Item Storage Component or 64k ME Item Storage Component. 256 Energy is required to generate a Matter Ball, 256,000 respecitvely for the
Singularity.
