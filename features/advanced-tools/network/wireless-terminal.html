Wireless Terminal

After you linked up the Wireless Terminal in the ME Security Terminal, it grants a portable access to the ME Network. Put it into a Charger to recharge it.
Holds 1.6m AE in its battery and drains 1 AE/t for each block you are away from the nearest ME Wireless Access Point.
