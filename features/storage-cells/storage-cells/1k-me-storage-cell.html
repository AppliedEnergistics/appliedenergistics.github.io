Lowest Tier Storage Cell, which can contain 1,024 bytes of storage.

1,024 bytes of storage can hold 127 Stacks of a single item. or 65 Stacks,while holding 63 Different items.

The 1k Storage Cell uses 8 bytes of data to store a single type. Click here for details on how storage math works.

When placed inside a drive or chest will consume 0.5 ae/t.

Must be in a ME Drive or ME Chest to be usable.

You can remove the 1k ME Item Storage Component by fully emptying the storage cell, and sneak clicking i in your hand.

The settings can be changed in the Cell Workbench.
