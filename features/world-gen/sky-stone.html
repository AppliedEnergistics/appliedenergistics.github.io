Sky Stone is a blast resistant dark stone block found in meteorites that have impacted the surface in the distant or semi recent past. Their origin is unknown however they appear to contain remnant of technology from another place.Sky Stone

is extremely hard and requires at least a diamond pick to mine it, the processed variants can be removed with any pick however.

Can be ground into Sky Stone Dust.

### Meteorites

Meteorites spawn during world generation on the surface or underground and vary in size. They generally contain a Sky Stone Chest which can contain various ingredients required for advanced technology. You can use a Meteorite Compass to locate meteorites near you and as you explore.
