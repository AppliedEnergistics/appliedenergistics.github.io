Acceleration Card

Can be installed into ME IO Port,
ME Item Import Bus and ME Item Export Bus to increase the speed of items being transfered, this also increases the idle drain of the machine by 1 ae/t per upgrade.
You can also insert Acceleration Card into Matter Cannon to increase the number of projectiles shot per usage, this cuases the device to use additional power for each bullet however.
