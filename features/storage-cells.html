Storage Cells

Storage Cells, are one of the core mechanics of storage in Applied Energistics 2, there are two kinds, one for quantities of items, and one for regions of space. Item storage cells can hold up to 63 distinct types of items; the number of items they can store depends in part on how many types they're holding; see also ME Storage Math.
