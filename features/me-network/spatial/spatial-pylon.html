Spatial Pylon

The main block used to construct a [Spatial Containment Structure](../Spatial- Containment-Structure/index.html), they must be built in straight lines, with a minium length of 2. When powered and on a network they will either glow a light purple, or a light red, if the color is light red, that indicates design has an issue and needs to be adjusted. They emitt a small amount of light when powered.
Only useful when used in conjunction with a Spatial IO Port, All Spatial Pylon in an [ME Network](../ME- Network/index.html) are part of the came [SCS](../Spatial-Containment- Structure/index.html).
Each Spatial Pylon Multiblock requires a channel ( 1 per strand of blocks, not 1 per Spatial Pylon ) to function.
