Energy Acceptor

The
Energy Acceptor converts energy from external systems into AE and stores it into the [ME Network](../ME- Network/index.html).
Currently supported energy conversions are:
IndustrialCraft 2
1 EU = 2 AE
Thermal Expansion 3
2 RF = 1 AE
RotaryCraft
11256 Watts/Joules = 1 AE
Mekanism
5 J = 1 AE
