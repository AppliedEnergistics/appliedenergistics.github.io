16k ME Storage Cell

Middle Tier Storage Cell, which can contain 16,384 bytes of storage.
16,384 bytes of storage can hold 2,032 Stacks of a single item. or 1,040 Stacks,while holding 63 Different items.
The 16k Storage Cell uses 128 bytes of data to store a single type. Click here for details on how storage math works.
When placed inside a drive or chest will consume 1.5 ae/t.
Must be in a ME Drive or ME Chest to be usable.
You can remove the 16k ME Storage Component by fully emptying the storage cell, and sneak clicking i in your hand.
