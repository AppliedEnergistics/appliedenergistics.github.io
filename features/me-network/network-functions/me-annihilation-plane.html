ME Annihilation Plane

The ME Item Annihilation Plane is a part designed to destroy any block put in front of it. It can buffer a single operation. Buffered items will automatically be stored in [ME Network](../ME- Network/index.html)'s Storage as space is made available.
Requires a channel to function.
