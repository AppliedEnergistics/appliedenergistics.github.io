The ME Item Formation Plane is a part designed to place or drop any item put into the [ME Network](../ME- Network/index.html). It functions as storage similar to how a ME Item Storage Bus, so items that are added to the network are dropped or placed passively. You can configure the ME Item Formation Plane to indicate specific items it should place. Items are placed instantly when they enter the network.

Requires a channel to function.
