ME Covered Cable

Covered cable variant, offers no real benefits over its Fluix ME Glass Cable counter part, however can be used as an alternate aesthetic choice if you prefer the covered look.
Can be colored in the same manner as Fluix ME Glass Cable. Four Fluix ME Covered Cable can be crafted with redstone and glowstone to make Fluix ME Dense Covered Cable.
